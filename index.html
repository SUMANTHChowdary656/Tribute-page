Steve Jobs

1955-2011

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like.Design is how it works"

Biography

Steve Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, and was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs. Raised in Silicon Valley, he developed an early interest in electronics and technology. In 1976, he co-founded Apple Inc. with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, introducing revolutionary products like the Apple II and Macintosh. After being ousted from Apple in 1985, he founded NeXT Inc. and later acquired Pixar, turning it into a leading animation studio. Apple acquired NeXT in 1997, bringing Jobs back to the company, where he became CEO. Under his leadership, Apple launched groundbreaking products such as the iPod, iPhone, and iPad. Jobs was known for his visionary thinking, emphasis on design, and powerful marketing skills. His work redefined consumer technology and digital media. Despite facing health challenges, he remained a driving force in the tech world. Steve Jobs passed away on October 5, 2011, but his legacy continues to shape modern innovation.
